# Business Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard

## Objective
Analyze business sales data to identify revenue trends, top-selling products, high-value categories, and regional performance.

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- Microsoft Excel

## Skills Applied
- Data Cleaning
- KPI Analysis
- Business Analytics
- Data Visualization
- Insight Generation

## Key Insights
- Technology generated the highest sales (~4.7M)
- Consumer segment contributed ~51% of total sales
- Central region generated the highest sales performance
- Overall profit margin was 11.61%
- Sales increased towards the end of the year

## Recommendations
- Focus marketing efforts on Technology products
- Increase marketing in low-performing regions
- Improve profitability in the Furniture category
- Target Consumer segment through loyalty programs
